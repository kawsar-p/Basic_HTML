SUDOKU CHALLENGE

Test your logic with this classic number puzzle

How to Play

  • Fill the grid so every row, column, and 3x3 box contains digits 1-9
  • Use the number buttons or keyboard to fill cells
  • Click 'Check' to verify your solution
  • Use 'Hint' to reveal a correct number
  • Start with Easy and progress to harder difficulties
